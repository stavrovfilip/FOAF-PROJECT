About FOAF

FOAF - Friend of a Friend is a machine readable ontology, which main purpose is to describe people, their activities and their relationships with other people.

FOAF is free and available to anyone!

With FOAF, people can describe their social networks, without needing a centralized database. FOAF is expressed using the RDF - Resource Description Framework and OWL- Web Ontology Language.
